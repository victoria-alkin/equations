How to Play

Fill in the blank boxes so the equation is true.

1. Click a blank box to select it (green border).

2. Click an operator button to place it. Focus moves to the next box automatically.

3. Press Check when all boxes are filled.

On harder levels, some boxes sit before a number (like √9) or after it (like 3²). Only the matching operator type will work — the other buttons will be dimmed when that box is selected.

Reset clears your answers. Skip goes to the next puzzle.
