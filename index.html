How to Play

Daily: Fill blank boxes so the equation is true. Drag an operator from the palette and drop it onto a box. Drag between boxes to rearrange, or click a filled box to clear it. One puzzle per difficulty per day.

Timed: Solve as many equations as you can in 60 seconds! Drag an operator onto a blank box (or between boxes to rearrange). Drop on the palette row to remove an operator from the equation. Correct answers advance automatically. Sign in to submit your score to the leaderboard.

Battle: Challenge a friend! Create a battle, send them the link, and once you both join you'll race through the same puzzles in the same order for 60 seconds. Highest score wins!

Badges: Finish the day in the top 3 of the daily leaderboard to earn a medal. for 1st, for 2nd, for 3rd. Badges appear next to your name on the leaderboard and on your profile.

Reset clears your answers. A new set of daily puzzles arrives every day!
