DRAG TO PLACE

How to Play

Fill in the blank boxes so the equation is true.

1. Drag an operator from the palette at the bottom.

2. Drop it onto any blank box in the equation.

3. To move an operator, drag it from its box to another. To remove one, drag it back to the palette area or click it.

4. Press Check when all boxes are filled.

On harder levels, some boxes sit next to a single number — those need operators like √ or ². Experiment!

Reset clears everything. Skip goes to the next puzzle.
