How to Play

You'll see an equation with blank boxes between the numbers. Your job is to fill in the correct math operators so the equation is true.

1. Click a blank box to select it (it turns green).

2. Click an operator button (+ − × ÷) to place it in the selected box. The next blank box is automatically selected.

3. Hit Check when all boxes are filled to see if you're right.

Reset clears your answers. Skip moves to the next puzzle. A new set of puzzles is available every day!
