How to Play

Daily: Fill blank boxes so the equation is true. Drag an operator from the palette and drop it onto a box. Drag between boxes to rearrange, or click a filled box to clear it. One puzzle per difficulty per day.

Timed: Solve as many equations as you can in 60 seconds! Click an operator to place it in the next empty box. Correct answers advance automatically. Sign in to submit your score to the leaderboard.

Reset clears your answers. A new set of daily puzzles arrives every day!
